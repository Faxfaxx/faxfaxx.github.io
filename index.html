what is minecraft?

Minecraft is a popular open-world video game created by Mojang in 2011. It is known for its blocky graphics, where players can explore, gather resources, build structures, and face creatures in a vast, procedurally generated world. There are several game modes, with the main ones being Survival mode, where players must manage resources and avoid dying from hunger or attacks, and Creative mode, which offers unlimited resources and the ability to build without limits.
